Dumb Ways to Die Discord Theme 

    For this theme, YOU get to choose how OTHER members are likely to DIE. 
    Drag their name from the member list onto the death that best fits (including your own name).
    You can add as many members to a death as you like.
    You can press on a name to remove it and add it back to the member list.
    
    Just to clarify a few things:-
    
    1) DON'T DO THIS FORM ON YOUR PHONE, IT WILL NOT WORK.

    2) YOU are voting for OTHER people's roles. You are assigning their role for them by choosing a death most befitting.
    
    3) IF YOURE EVER CONFUSED, PLEASE REFER TO THE DESCRIPTION BOX ON THE SIDE. IF YOURE STILL CONFUSED @SAMI
    
    4) If you dont like any of the roles or have a better suggestion, PLEASE type your suggestions in the other field at the bottom <3
